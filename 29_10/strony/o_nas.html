About Shadow

Shadow is the "Ultimate Lifeform", created aboard the Space Colony ARK over fifty years ago by Professor Gerald Robotnik

He is the final result of Project Shadow, a government experiment that was only completed by infusing Shadow with the DNA of the alien Black Doom.

Fueled by vengeance, Shadow fought Sonic and friends until his true memories of Maria returned, inspiring him to save the world from Gerald's doomsday plot.
